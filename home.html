F.A.B.R.I.C. is a student-run project about sustainable fashion and textile recycling.About 92 million tonnes of textile waste gets dumped in landfills every year. We're here to talk about that, and what we can do differently.

What We're About

The Problem

Fast fashion is the second-largest polluting industry on the planet. The average person throws out about 31 kg of clothing a year, and synthetic fabrics sit in landfill for 200 years leaking microplastics into soil and water.

Our Goal

We want people on campus thinking about where their clothes come from and where they end up. Thrifting, upcycling, swapping — there are loads of alternatives to buying new stuff every time you want something to wear.

Take Action

Curious how your habits stack up? Head over to the Impact Simulator and pick the sustainable habits you already do. You might be making more of a difference than you think.
